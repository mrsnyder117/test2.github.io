Sofia Magill's Print Project
                             

Bookmark

For this project, we made 2"x 8" bookmarks for a local elementary school in InDesign. It had to be in single color ink as we would be printing using a lithographic printer. In Lithography, your design is chemically created on a metal plate. Then the plate is placed in the printer and an oil-based ink is applied. Since water and oil don't mix, the ink only sticks to the area that has your design. Our designs had to be strictly line work, so no photos could be used.
